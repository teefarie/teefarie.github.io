What if an apartment found you instead?

An easy and better experience to get an apartment or flatmate.

Background

People move daily to and within Lagos which means a lot of them will either be searching for apartments to live in or people to live with but unfortunately, this has been a very big problem that the current system hasn't been able to solve conveniently.

It's very stressful and time-consuming to get the kind of apartment you want in Lagos. Even though there are a lot of property listing sites, having to go check out 10, 15 to 20 apartments or changing multiple agents before eventually settling for one house is draining.

What if instead of finding an apartment, it found you?

After experiencing this for the third time, I decided to design an easy and better experience from a perspective of the person who is looking for an apartment as well as one who is looking for a flatmate.

Ps - This is not a Lagos only thing. It's a very common problem happening everywhere. After seeing the search result of some keywords on Twitter, I realized it's something worth attempting.

  • "I'm looking for a flatmate"
  • "looking for an apartment"
