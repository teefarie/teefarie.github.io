We’re looking for Product Thinkers

We hire great designers to work on creating the most intuitive and frictionless applications.

How to join our talent pool

We are granular when it comes to picking who we work with. Our vetting process ensures we get the best to work on delicate projects.

Stage 1 - Application & First screening

Candidate needs to have a good portfolio, highlighting the best projects they have worked on. We would love to see how they approach solving problems, think of a solution and how translated it into a beautiful design.

Stage 3 - Phone conversation

This conversation is to get to know the designer, gain insight into the their thought process. It’s another opportunity to ask the designer about their portfolio and also talk about their work.

Stage 3 - Take home exercise

The designer is given a product design exercise for us to access their approach to design.

Stage 4 - Second screening

A phone conversation to talk about the exercise and understand how the designer thought about the problem and what approach and decision was taken to solve it.

Stage 5 - Small project

Before the designer is added to the pool, we test the designer by putting them on a small real life project and see how they perform.

Stage 6 - Acceptance and on boarding

This conversation is to talk to the designer about our current product design process at Devcenter, tools we use and what we expect.
