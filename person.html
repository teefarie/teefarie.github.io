I'm Fari, a Product Designer with over 5 years experience working with startups to build products, better processes and systems to enable people and enhance businesses.

I am passionate about building products people love and great companies but also very interested in the thoughts, processes, systems, and strategies that will power and enable them so as to scale and grow them the right way in the future.

My expertise includes; User Research, Design Thinking, Product and Business Strategy, Product Management, UX Design, Visual (UI) Design, Prototyping, Usability Testing, Front-end Design. (Well for now)

I am really excited to work with super smart and passionate people who want to solve hard/complex problems, design useful/usable products and also build great companies that will challenge the status quo.
