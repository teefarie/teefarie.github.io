Hey, I’m Fari, a Product Designer with over 5 years of experience in building digital products.

I have previously worked with small-scale startups and agencies where I have designed, developed, and launched products. I have led design teams and got involved in the processes of scaling a business. Also, I once tried and failed at launching a successful product of my own.

I enjoy exploring various ways to solve problems and being involved in the whole process of following up ideas from concept to execution by leveraging the power of design thinking, technology, and business. I design with Sketch and code front-end with HTML, CSS (LESS/SASS), and JS (for interactions / DOM manipulation only).

My love for design thinking and improving the way people people do things, live, or behave led my friends and I to start writing about different daily experiences that can be enhanced with design thinking on himhimandher.

Some of my favorite things to do are lifting weights (I wrote an article here about how lifting weights helped me become a better designer), cooking (I have a track record of cooking the best jollof), eating (I love food), and listening to great music which I perfectly enjoy doing in a cold dark room.
