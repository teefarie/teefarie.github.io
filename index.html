We built this because managing developer portfolios on excel sheets was becoming a nightmare, true story. We believe developers in our ecosystem should have a simple way of telling you how good they are and work they’ve done. While this is beta, it is beginning of many nice things to come.

Companies apply to you, not the other way around.

We just want you to say the most important things.

More doesn’t mean better. By telling us three simple things, we’ll help you craft a portfolio that will make you stand out.

  • Skills

    The languages you know, tools you use, your years of experience, and what you have done with them.

  • Work Preference

    The type of work you want, how much you want to earn, the exact type of role you want and where you want to work from.

  • Recommendations

    Recommendations from people you’ve worked with increases your chances of receiving offers.
