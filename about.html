We are building Africa’s finest network of Software Developers & Designers.


The world as we know it, is changing very quickly; the way the most advanced economies developed a decade ago is a complete departure from what is obtainable now. Human race which was transformed by manufacturing, is seeing a tectonic shift and to thrive in this new world order is to acknowledge three factors:

The rising demand for Knowledge Workers: these are people whose main capital is knowledge, for example; software engineers, design thinkers, public accountants and so on. More than ever, there’s an increasing demand for a capabale knowledge workforce with skills that transcends geographical boundaries.

The Transformation of the Nature of Work: The idea of productive work being done within the four walls of an office environment is dead. More creative professionals are opting for freedom and flexibility to innovate within their own space. A new mindset towards working with the best is required to have a competitive advantage.

The role of Technology: Technology is probably the most important; for one, it has reduced the barrier of access to opportunity. Tech has enabled automation of processes allowing us to achieve efficiency and deliver quality. And it will continue to shape how we work.

Having the knowledge of the above is what drives us. Close to our hearts, is a mission to connect a generation of knowledge workers to opportunity to create a true digital economy, starting with software development.
