Our mission is to enable market participants in the Sub-Saharan Africa real estate market.

Whether you are utilising our online application or prefer a more customised service through interaction with the ei Advisory team, we provide our users with the research and data they require to successfully navigate Sub-Saharan Africa’s opaque real estate market to make informed business decisions.
