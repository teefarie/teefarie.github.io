Data Driven Design Process

Our entire process involves brainstorming, defining, and developing your idea.

Part 1 - Collective Brainstorming

This is a conversation concerning the target users, expected user interactions within the app as well as the business pain points.

Part 2 - UX Research, User Journey, and Storyboarding

The team placed on the project breaks down the solution into user journeys and sketches.

Part 3 - Wireframing

We lay out ideas on how the product should work and look.

Part 4 - User Testing

We give your potential users clickable wireframes to validate your idea.

Part 5 - High Fidelity Design and Acceptance Criteria

We review the final prototype and create achievable sprints for the software developers and testers.

Part 6 - Pushed to Development

Software development starts and the process is constantly guided by the project manager.

De-risked Product Development

We have designed our product development process to be simple yet flexible enough to respond to changing priorities.

  • We break down each project into achievable milestones.

  • We create a delivery timeline approved by all parties

  • The Project Manager communicates weekly achievements to the client.

  • Work done is pushed to a test-engineer who assures it is up to our acceptance criteria.
