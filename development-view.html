Sogenal Tower, Alfred Rewane (Kingsway) Road, Ikoyi – Lagos.

By Dolapo Omidire at December 7, 2016 1 minute

Sogenal Tower is a 15 floor office development located on the corner of Cameron and Alfred Rewane Road. It is being developed by Eris Property Group and Sogenal Group, and was designed by Boogertman & Partners Architects in collaboration with James Cubitt Architects. Other members of the project team include Profica, MOA, KOA, Tillyard Consultants and more.

According to Eris, it will offer 11,560m² of lettable office space and has been designed to achieve a 4 Star rated Green Building in accordance with GBCSA’s (Green Building Council of South Africa) Green Star Office Rating tool.

In July 2016, the project was launched by Eris and Sogenal and is now open to inquiries for new tenants.

The site for the project is on the N1/Accra-Tema Motorway/George W Bush Highway and is in close proximity with Kotoka International Airport, Accra Mall and Fiesta Royale Hotel. The Ghana National Petroleum Corporation and Tullow Oil that are located in the neighbourhood also mean the region is often regarded as the oil and gas hub of Accra.
