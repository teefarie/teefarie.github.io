FAQs

  • What is Estate Intel?

    Estate Intel is Africa’s largest online commercial property database. Our mission is to enable real estate market participants make informed business decisions through the use of our digital channels.

  • What does Estate Intel do?

    We provide market research, data and other services.

  • Can you help me apply for a job in this development?

    Even though we have provided information for a particular development or building, we may not be in direct contact with the building owners. Please visit their website to reach out to them.

  • Why is a development project on your website?

    There are a number of people who require this information and we are publishing it here to enable.

  • Can you help me find a house?

    We do not offer agency services.

  • Can you tell me the opening times of a particular building?

    Even though we have provided information for a particular development or building, we may not be in direct contact with the building owners. Please visit their website to find out more information about them.

  • Can I speak to someone on your team?

    Send an email to team@estateintel.com and or visit the About Us page and reach out to one of us directly and we will be in touch with you shortly.

  • I would like to request if you have the data I’m looking for?

    Send an email to team@estateintel.com and or visit the About Us page and reach out to one of us directly and we will be in touch with you shortly.

  • How do you get your data and is it trustworthy?

    Our team has spent time a significant amount of time gathering data and doing a substantial amount of leg work over the past few years. We retrieve our information directly from those involved with assets or transactions to avoid dilution or information misinterpretation. If you are interested in becoming a subscriber to estate intel and would like more information, please send an email to team@estateintel.com or visit the About Us page and reach out to one of us directly and we will be in touch with you shortly.

  • Can I submit data or a new story?

    Yes! You can submit data to submit@estateintel.com and a member of our team will reach out to you to begin our verification process. You are welcome to drop the data or a news story anonymously but we encourage you to leave your details in case we have questions. If we are unable to verify or reach you, the data or news content may not be published.
