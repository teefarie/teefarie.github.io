Connecting thousands of developers and designers all over Africa

Over the years, we have reached out and connected more developers, designers, and project managers to an ever expanding network to foster software development excellence.

Amazing community network

Through the process of curating projects, stories and ideas from African developers, designers, and project managers, we created a unique community online and offline to solve problems within the ecosystem.

  • Knowledge Sharing

    Knowledge Sharing

    Ours is a community that is more connected than the average. Members are always open to helping others with the skills and knowledge they have gained over the years.

  • Collaboration

    Collaboration

    Two heads are better than one might be a big cliché, but it nonetheless holds true for what happens in the community. Whenever there is a call for help or a need to build a solution, members work together as a team.

  • Mentoring

    Mentoring

    Members willingly want to help others be as good or even better than they are. As a new designer/developer, you can tap into this robust knowledge base to become better at what you do and earn more in the process.

  • Opportunities

    Opportunities

    The community is a pool of opportunities and this creates the environment for members to find new job opportunities, meet their future co-founders and also learn about the latest trends in software development.
