Get a product requirement document

UX Research and Prototyping

Every product design starts with a sketch. We then produce a prototype - something we can get the target user to test before we begin actual software development. It is the lowest risk point of starting any project, before committing hours of development time.

  • User research interviews

    We find your ideal audience and ask them a couple of questions before we begin to design your application.

  • Build wireframes

    We proceed to build wireframes. It helps us organise features and gives you a visual representation of your idea.

  • Develop a prototype

    We develop a lo-fi prototype to test and validate your idea.
